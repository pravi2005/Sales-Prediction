Signal Intelligence

Four dimensions of
purchase prediction

01
Screen Time Analysis
Daily device engagement patterns reveal intent and digital comfort. High screen time users exhibit 2.3× greater conversion probability when combined with session depth signals.
02
Demographic Scoring
Age and income brackets create nuanced purchasing power profiles. Our model weights these against historical cohort data from 2.4M customer journeys to calibrate real intent.
03
Platform Session Depth
Time-on-platform per session is a leading indicator of purchase intent. Customers spending 15+ minutes browse to evaluate — a key signal our model treats with premium weight.
04
Behavioral Frequency
Weekly session frequency maps habit formation. Returning users who visit 5+ times per week are in a pre-decision loop — our engine flags these as high-value conversion windows.
05
Purchase History Weight
Loyalty signals compound probability. A user with 3+ prior orders has a 78% baseline conversion rate. The model amplifies or discounts new signals against this prior history.
06
Device Context
Mobile users convert on impulse; desktop users research before committing. Device context shifts the probability curve and determines optimal intervention timing for your CRM.
